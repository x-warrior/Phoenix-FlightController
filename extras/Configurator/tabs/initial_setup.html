Calibrate ESCs

Requesting ESC calibration will result in ESC throttle range calibration on next flight controller startup.
Its recommended to remove propellers before calibrating (for safety).

Calibrate Accelerometer

Place your UAV on a flat surface (properly leveled desk works great) and click on the calibration button.
Try not to bump into the desk while calibration is running.
Calibration will take approximately 2 seconds, you can view the raw output from calibration in console.
You can view the calibration "effectivity" in 3D Vehicle tab.

Initialize EEPROM

This step is mostly "optional" as Flight Controller automatically re-initializes the EEPROM after firmware upload.
In case you encounter "weird" values/behaviour in the PID Tuning section, this is the button to press.
Re-powering the Flight Controller afterwards is recommended.
